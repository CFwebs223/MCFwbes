# MCF.webs — Daily Growth Playbook

Execute these DAILY for consistent client acquisition.

---

## 6:00 AM — Content Creation (45 min)

**Mon/Wed/Fri** — Short-form video day
- Record 3 videos (15-30 sec each) using hooks from viral-hooks.md
- Edit in CapCut/InShot with trending audio
- Post to TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts

**Tue/Thu** — Carousel + Thread day
- Create 1 carousel post (Canva) for Instagram/LinkedIn
- Write 1 Twitter/X thread (10-15 tweets)
- Schedule via Buffer/Hootsuite

**Saturday** — Weekly showcase
- Create 1 cinematic website demo video
- Post across all platforms

**Sunday** — Strategy + analytics
- Review weekly performance
- Plan next week's content
- Update lead tracking

## 8:00 AM — Lead Generation (30 min)

Use the lead targeting criteria to find 20 new leads:
- **Instagram**: Search "#capetownbusiness" "#restaurant" "#smallbizsa" — DM 10
- **LinkedIn**: Search "CEO" "owner" "founder" + industry — connect + message
- **Google Maps**: Find local businesses with outdated web presence — find contact info

Track every lead in the spreadsheet.

## 9:00 AM — Outreach Batch #1 (45 min)

- Send 10 cold DMs (Instagram/LinkedIn)
- Send 5 cold emails
- Make 5 cold calls
- Follow up on 5 previous outreach attempts

Use the scripts from outreach-system.md. Personalize each.

## 10:00 AM — Client Work (flexible)

- Work on active projects
- Record progress for content
- Take screenshots/video for case studies

## 12:00 PM — Outreach Batch #2 (30 min)

- Send 5 more DMs
- Send 5 more emails
- Make 3 more calls
- Reply to any inbound inquiries

## 2:00 PM — Content Engagement (30 min)

- Engage with 30 posts in your target audience (comment, like, share)
- Reply to every comment on your posts
- DM anyone who engages with your content
- Post 3 stories on Instagram (behind-the-scenes, tips, polls)

## 4:00 PM — Follow-ups (30 min)

- Follow up on all pending leads
- Send proposals to qualified leads
- Confirm any scheduled calls
- Update lead statuses

## 5:00 PM — Daily Review (15 min)

- What worked today?
- What didn't?
- What content performed best?
- Update this playbook based on learnings
