## 1. Brand Voice & Positioning

> "mcf.webs gives South African businesses a web presence that doesn't embarrass them. AI-built, human-refined, and built to convert — without the agency price tag or the DIY trash."

### Voice Chart

| Dimension | Approach |

- "You don't need a R50k website. You need a R5k website that works better."

## 2. Platform-by-Platform Strategy

---

### 2.1 TikTok

**Primary Goal:** Reach SA business owners aged 25-45 with high-impact, low-attention-span content.  
**Secondary Goal:** Viral reach through relatable SA business pain points.  
**Tone:** Fast-paced, direct, slightly chaotic. Hook in under 2 seconds.

#### Content Focus
- "Before & After" agency transformations (screen recordings with voiceover)
- SA-specific business owner frustrations (Load shedding killed my online orders. Again.)
- AI workflow speed content (I built this website in 14 minutes — here's the proof.)
- Restaurant/hospitality focus: digital menu reveals, online ordering demos
- "Rate my website" roasting series — roast bad SA business sites (with blurred names)

#### Format Preferences
- Vertical 9:16, 15-45 seconds
- Caption-heavy (SA users often watch on mute in meetings)
- Fast cuts, trending sounds where appropriate
- Text overlay for every key stat

#### Specific Content Pillar Alignment on TikTok
1. **Speed builds** (capability pillar) → "Watch me build a restaurant site in 12 mins"
2. **Roasts** (education pillar) → "Your website is costing you customers"
3. **Before/After** (social proof pillar) → "We fixed this Joburg deli's online ordering"
4. **SA-specific** (culture pillar) → "Load shedding proof website design"

#### Viral Hooks for TikTok (SA-specific)
- "Every SA business owner needs to hear this."
- "Stop paying R15k for a website your cousin can build in Wix."
- "Your restaurant is losing orders RIGHT NOW and you don't even know."
- "I built a better website than [SA Brand] in 15 minutes."
- "3 things wrong with every South African small business website."

### 2.2 Instagram

**Primary Goal:** Build brand trust and showcase design quality through visual storytelling.  
**Secondary Goal:** Drive DMs and link clicks for consultations.  
**Tone:** Polished but not sterile. More design-forward than TikTok.

#### Content Focus
- **Reels (60% of posts):** Same high-energy TikTok content, re-purposed with Instagram-native editing
- **Carousels (25% of posts):** "5 signs your website is costing you customers" — swipeable value
- **Static Posts (10% of posts):** Portfolio showcases, before/after screenshots
- **Stories (Daily):** Quick tips, client wins, polls ("What's your current website builder?"), Q&A

#### Content Buckets for Instagram

| Bucket | Frequency | Format |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Portfolio Showcase | 2x/week | Carousel (3-5 slides showing one site) |
| SA Business Education | 1x/week | Carousel or Reel |
| Behind the Scenes | 1x/week | Reel (building a site, client convo) |
| Social Proof | 1x/week | Testimonial graphic + screenshot |
| Culture / Personality | 1x/week | Meme, SA business joke, relatable |

#### Instagram-Specific Tactics
- Use Instagram's "Add Yours" sticker for engagement
- Post carousels with "Save this for later" CTAs
- Collaborative posts with SA business pages (restaurants we've built for)
- Story highlights: "Portfolio", "Pricing", "Process", "Reviews", "Free Tools"

### 2.3 LinkedIn

**Primary Goal:** Build credibility as the go-to web agency for serious SA business owners.  
**Secondary Goal:** Generate B2B leads (restaurant groups, retail chains, service businesses).  
**Tone:** More professional but still direct. Less humor, more authority.

#### Content Focus
- **Thought Leadership Posts (3x/week):** Insights on SA digital economy, website ROI, AI in web design
- **Case Studies (1x/week):** Detailed breakdown of a specific client project
- **Industry Commentary:** React to SA tech news, Google algorithm changes, digital trends
- **Long-form Posts:** 800-1500 word posts that establish expertise
- **Document posts (PDF carousels):** "The SA Business Owner's Website Checklist"

#### LinkedIn Specific Tactics
- Tag clients (with permission) in case study posts
- Comment on posts from SA business influencers to build network
- Use LinkedIn's "Create a Newsletter" feature for weekly web tips
- Post in SA small business / entrepreneurship groups
- Leverage LinkedIn Polls: "What's stopping you from upgrading your website?"

#### Content Calendar for LinkedIn (Weekly Rhythm)

| Day | Content Type | Topic |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Monday | Motivation + Strategy | "Your website should be your hardest worker" |
| Tuesday | Case Study | Client site launch / results breakdown |
| Wednesday | Industry Insight | AI in web design, Google updates, SA trends |
| Thursday | Educational Carousel | How-to, checklist, framework |
| Friday | Lighter / Culture | SA business owner meme, wins, community |

### 2.4 X / Twitter

**Primary Goal:** Real-time engagement, industry authority, and personality-driven brand building.  
**Secondary Goal:** Traffic to website via threaded content and link posts.  
**Tone:** Fast, witty, hot takes, short-form value.

#### Content Focus
- **Hot Takes:** Opinions on SA web design, tech, and business
- **Threads:** Deep dives into specific topics (5-10 tweets)
- **Micro-Tips:** Single tweet value bombs
- **Engagement Baits:** Polls, questions, "agree or disagree?"
- **Reaction Content:** Reply to viral SA business tweets with web design perspective

#### X-Specific Tactics
- Use Twitter Communities (SA Business, SA Tech, Restaurant Owners)
- Quote-tweet bad website examples (constructive, not mean)
- Thread storm: "10 websites that made me RAGE as a web designer" (all SA examples)
- Pin the best-performing thread to profile
- Engage with #SmallBusinessSA and #SABusiness hashtag communities

#### Sample Tweet Styles
- "Hot take: If your SA business doesn't have mobile ordering in 2026, you're literally burning money. Here's why:"
- "Thread: I analyzed 50 South African small business websites. Here are the 5 things ALL of them get wrong."
- "Your website is loading in 6 seconds in a country where data costs R150/GB. Fix it."
- "SA business owners: stop putting 'email us for a quote' as your only CTA. You're losing 70% of leads."

**Primary Goal:** Capture search traffic for "website for [SA city] [business type]" queries.

## 4. Viral Hook Formulas for Web Design

### Formula 1: The "You're Losing Money" Hook
**Structure:** [Stat about loss] + [Specific audience] + [Turnaround]
- "Your restaurant is losing R20,000/month in missed online orders. Here's why."
- "Every second your website takes to load costs you one customer. Here's the fix."
- "You're paying for Google Ads but sending traffic to a 2015 website. Stop."

### Formula 2: The "Stop Doing X" Hook
**Structure:** Stop [bad behavior] + [Because reason] + [Do this instead]
- "Stop letting your cousin build your website. Here's what it's costing you."
- "Stop using 'Contact Us' as your only call to action. Here's what to use instead."
- "Stop copying your competitor's website. Here's why you're losing."

### Formula 3: The "I Fixed X in Y Minutes" Hook
**Structure:** I [action] + [specific thing] + in [time] + [result]
- "I rebuilt this Joburg cafe's entire online menu system in 22 minutes."
- "I fixed this SA bakery's broken booking system in 15 minutes."
- "I redesigned this Durban clothing store's site in 30 minutes. Sales tripled."

### Formula 4: The "South Africa Specific" Hook
**Structure:** [SA reality] + [web impact] + [solution]
- "Load shedding schedule + your website = disaster. Here's the fix."
- "Data costs in SA are R150/GB. Your website uses 12MB per visit. Fix it."
- "Capetonians are searching for your business right now. Can they find it?"

### Formula 5: The "Behind the Curtain" Hook
**Structure:** "I'm a [role] and [shocking truth]"
- "I'm a web designer and I'm telling you: 80% of SA business websites are broken."
- "I build AI websites for a living. Here's what the industry doesn't want you to know."
- "I've seen 100+ SA business websites this year. Most of them are unprofessional."

### Formula 6: The "Challenge / Dare" Hook
**Structure:** "I bet [audience] can't [challenge]"
- "I bet your website isn't mobile-optimized. Prove me wrong in the comments."
- "I bet your restaurant menu page takes longer to load than your food takes to cook."
- "I dare you to check your Google PageSpeed score. Screenshot or it didn't happen."

### Formula 7: The "Numbers Don't Lie" Hook
**Structure:** [Number] + [shocking fact] + [your solution]
- "1.2 seconds. That's how long you have before a mobile user leaves. Your site loads in 6."
- "94% of first impressions are design-related. Your site looks like it was built in 2010."
- "70% of SA small businesses have no online ordering. That's 70% leaving money on the table."

## 6. Posting Cadence

### Optimal Schedule

| Platform | Posts/Week | Best Times (SAST) | Best Days |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| **TikTok** | 5-7 | 6-8am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm | Mon, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat |
| **Instagram (Reels + Posts)** | 4-5 | 7-9am, 12:30pm, 7:30pm | Mon-Fri (skip Sun) |
| **Instagram Stories** | Daily | Throughout day | Every day |
| **LinkedIn** | 5 | 6-8am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm | Mon-Fri (peak Tue-Thu) |
| **X/Twitter** | 3-5 | 7am, 12pm, 6pm | Mon-Fri, Sat optional |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 3 | 9am, 3pm | Mon, Wed, Fri |
| **YouTube Long-form** | 1/month | 10am | First Thursday of month |

### Rationale
- **Monday morning:** Business owners are planning their week — hit them with strategy/value
- **Wednesday:** Highest engagement across platforms for B2B content
- **Thursday/Friday:** Restaurant/hospitality owners preparing for weekend — show them digital menu/booking solutions
- **Saturday:** Lower competition, SA business owners scrolling while relaxing
- **Sunday:** Skipped for most platforms (lowest engagement)

### Minimum Viable Cadence (for solo founder phase)
- TikTok: 3x/week
- Instagram: 3x/week (mix Reels + Carousels)
- LinkedIn: 2x/week
- X: 2x/week
- YouTube: 2 Shorts/week

## 8. Engagement Tactics

### Daily Engagement Routine (15-30 min/day)

**Morning (5 min):**
- Reply to all comments on yesterday's posts
- Reply to all DMs / messages

**Midday (10 min):**
- Find 5 posts in your niche — leave valuable comments (not "Great post!")
- Engage with SA business hashtags
- Repost/save relevant content

**Evening (5 min):**
- Check analytics for top-performing post
- Engage with followers who engaged with you

### Platform-Specific Engagement

**TikTok:**
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting (boosts algorithm)
- Use video replies to comments (high engagement rate)
- Stitch/duet viral SA content and add web design perspective
- Like and reply to comments on competitor videos

**Instagram:**
- Reply to DMs within 1 hour (creates algorithm boost)
- Use "question" sticker in Stories for Q&A
- Run "Ask Me Anything" on Stories weekly
- Send voice notes to engaged followers (personal touch)
- Share user-generated content from clients

**LinkedIn:**
- Comment on posts from SA business influencers before posting your own content
- Send personalized connection requests (no templated notes)
- Engage meaningfully in SA entrepreneur groups
- Tag relevant people in comments (not in post — looks spammy)
- Congratulate connections on work anniversaries, new roles, launches

**X/Twitter:**
- Reply to trending SA business tweets with your perspective
- Retweet with comment (adds value beyond just sharing)
- Create Twitter Spaces for monthly AMA sessions
- Participate in #SmallBusinessSA chat hours
- Direct message new followers with a value resource (not a sales pitch)

**YouTube:**
- Reply to every comment within 48 hours
- Pin the best comment on each video
- Use community tab for polls and updates
- Ask viewers to comment a specific word for a free resource
