# MCF.webs — Brand Positioning & Market Strategy

## Core Identity
- **Brand**: mcf.webs — AI-Built Websites & Digital Products
- **Tagline**: "Digital Experiences That Move Effortlessly"
- **Position**: Premium web development for businesses that refuse to settle for average
- **Vibe**: Cinematic, modern, elite, technically advanced, results-focused

## Target Audience (ICP)
1. **Restaurants & Cafés** — Need digital menus, online ordering, booking systems
2. **Service Professionals** — Coaches, therapists, consultants needing booking pages
3. **E-commerce Startups** — Lightweight online stores needing premium presentation
4. **Event Companies** — Landing pages, ticketing systems, immersive experiences
5. **Local Businesses** — Plumbers, electricians, gyms needing professional web presence

## Unique Value Props
- "Cinematic websites that convert — built in days, not months"
- "AI-assisted efficiency with human creative direction"
- "Premium quality at agency-level without agency prices"
- "Mobile-first, performance-optimized, conversion-focused"
